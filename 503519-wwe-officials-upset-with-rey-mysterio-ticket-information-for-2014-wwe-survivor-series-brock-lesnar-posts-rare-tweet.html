WWE Officials Upset with Mysterio

F4WOnline.com is reporting that WWE officials are extremely upset with Alberto Del Rio and Rey Mysterio right now for working with AAA promotion in Mexico.

Dave Meltzer noted on his Wrestling Observer Radio that WWE does not want to be painted as racist, especially to the huge Latino community since they have tried to build Latino superstars over the past few years, and especially since Rey Mysterio has stopped working full-time due to his injuries.

To read the full transcript of Alberto Del Rio basically calling WWE “racist” from AAA this past weekend, click here.

WWE Survivor Series Tickets

Tickets for the 2014 WWE Survivor Series will go on sale this Saturday at 11am EST. The tickets can be purchased through Ticketmaster.com. 

WWE Survivor Series will take place on November 23rd from the Scottrade Center in St. Louis, Missouri.
