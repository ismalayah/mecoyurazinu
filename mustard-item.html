Mustard (Japanese: マスタード Mustard) is a type of ingredient introduced in Generation IX.

In the core series games

Effect

It can be used as an ingredient in making a sandwich.

It adds the following modifiers to a sandwich it is used on:

  • 4 Sweet Taste
  • 8 Salty Taste
  • 8 Sour Taste
  • 16 Salt Taste
  • -3 Egg Power
  • 12 Exp. Power
  • 21 Raid Power
  • 2 Ground Type Power
  • 2 Rock Type Power

Description

SVA very spicy condiment that's indispensable when bread is on the table. It pairs tremendously with ketchup.
