How Tall is Trace Adkins?

Trace Adkins is one of the tallest country singers ever.

He stands at 6 feet 6 inches (198 cm) tall, which makes him tower over most of his peers and fans.

Trace Adkins Age

Trace Adkins was born on January 13, 1962, in Sarepta, Louisiana.

He is currently 61 years old as of August 2023.

Trace Adkins  grew up in a musical family and learned to play guitar from his father when he was 10 years old.

He joined a gospel group in high school and later attended Louisiana Tech University on a football scholarship.

Trace Adkins Net Worth

Trace Adkins has accumulated a net worth of $19 million as of 2023.

Trace Adkins has also appeared in several movies and TV shows, such as The Lincoln Lawyer, Monarch, and The Celebrity Apprentice.

Trace Adkins Wife
