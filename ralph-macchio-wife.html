Meet Phyllis Fierro, Cobra Kai Star Ralph Macchios Wife

Publish date: 2024-04-28

In Hollywood, long-lasting relationships are quite uncommon, but when they do happen, it’s lovely to witness. In addition, according to Country Living, Ralph Macchio has been wed to Phyllis Fierro for more than 30 years. On April 5, 1987, a few years after “The Outsiders” and the original “The Karate Kid,” the couple exchanged vows. However, they first connected at his cousin’s sweet sixteen in the basement of his grandmother almost ten years previously. The age of Macchio was fifteen.

According to Macchio, the setting of their first meeting was “this is 1970 or whatever, so this is Cheez Doodles, 7Up, Tootsie Rolls, maybe the Ruffles with onion dip.” She was my cousin’s buddy, and the only things we did were grin, chat, and dance a bit. Most likely the Hustle! Fierro, on the other hand, was already lost after their first encounter. According to People, Fierro stated in 2009, “I felt a natural affinity the first time I saw these large, chocolate brown eyes.” So who is Fierro, the partner Macchio has had in both his more recent appearances and the pinnacles of his career?

Phyllis Fierro, Ralph Macchio’s wife, was on the front lines of COVID

Phyllis Fierro, Ralph Macchio’s wife, is a nurse practitioner, contrary to the actor’s long career and brief appearance on “Dancing With The Stars,” which Country Living noted. Fierro has been extremely busy in the recent years due to the COVID-19 outbreak. During a January 2021 appearance on “The View,” Macchio remarked, “It’s tough.” She deals with the families and practises palliative care for a large portion of her work. She is a nurse practitioner, which means she works with patients’ families and end-of-life care. “I am really proud of her; it is hero work.” We’re still here, he stated of himself and his wife. The members of Macchio’s family are his wife and their two children, Julia and Daniel Macchio (yes, Daniel was given the name of Macchio’s well-known “The Karate Kid” character).

And it’s neither the first nor the only time he’s referred to his wife’s profession as heroic. He referred to Fierro as “one of the family heroes” while appearing on “The Drew Barrymore Show.” He continued to talk about her work, noting that she aids in “managing those really difficult decisions” that come with those who are approaching the end of their lives for the patients and their families. He told Barrymore, “It’s hero work, you know, really is.

Macchio credits his wife for keeping him grounded in Hollywood

You wonder what their secret is when you see someone who has been married for more than 30 years, like Ralph Macchio and Phyllis Fierro. How have they managed to maintain such a solid bond for so long? Well, Macchio explained that in order to make it work, both parties must believe that they are in the right place. It’s the conviction that we are supposed to be together through the good times and the bad, no matter what we go through, Macchio said to People (via Country Living).

Macchio added that his wife serves as his anchor while working as an actor in Hollywood. He admitted to having a “one foot in, one foot out” mentality when it comes to the entertainment industry, and a large part of that is due to Fierro. One foot in, one foot out would have been extremely tough for me to accomplish on my own without her and without the family we built together, according to Macchio. “Because balance wouldn’t exist.”
